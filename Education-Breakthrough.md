# Russia’s Breakthrough in Education: The Mission of the Great Teacher

> *"Then what are we fighting for?"* — Winston Churchill (on refusing to cut the education budget during WWII).

---

## I. Education as Strategic Capital
In a globalized world, competition is no longer just about resources; it is about the **Creative Personality**. As Eberhard von Kuenheim (former BMW Chairman) noted, true capital is formed through a combination of versatile instruction and the cultivation of a well-rounded individual.

### The Space Race Lesson
In the 20th century, the USSR won the space race not just with rockets, but "at the school desk," as U.S. President John F. Kennedy admitted. Today, the challenge remains the same: teaching students not just to **know**, but to **understand**.

---

## II. Global Models: From Class-Based to Merit-Based
We analyze various international approaches to identify the most effective path:
* **Switzerland & Germany:** Free access with a rigorous "first-year sieve" to select the intellectual elite.
* **Finland:** Shifting responsibility from bureaucrats to teachers. Teaching is a high-status, prestigious profession.
* **Turkey:** Providing free higher education for millions despite fewer natural resources than Russia.

---

## III. The Digital Frontier: INTUIT & Coursera
The Internet is the ultimate tool for democratizing "The Instructional Word." 
* Projects like **INTUIT** (created by A.V. Shkred) and **Coursera** prove that mass online education is a reality.
* **The Goal:** To create a symbiosis of classical school education and limitless digital platforms.

---

## IV. The Status of the Teacher
The greatest tragedy of modern education is the decline of the teacher's status to a "service provider." 

**Our Proposal:**
1. **De-commercialization:** Education must be removed from the market logic of "services."
2. **Teacher-University Tandem:** Educators, not bureaucrats, should define the standards of exams like the EGE.
3. **The Global Classroom:** Use the Internet to make the word of a "Great Teacher" accessible to millions.
