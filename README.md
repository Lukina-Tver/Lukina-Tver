# Aesthetics of Education & AI Video Studio 🎬

Welcome to the official repository of **Olga Lukina**, PhD in Philosophy and head of the "Aesthetics of Education" video studio (Tver, Russia). 

This space serves as an intellectual bridge between classical cultural heritage and the digital future. We believe that true education is a trinity of **Creative Personality, Instruction, and Upbringing.**

---

## 🌟 Our Mission
We create immersive educational and enlightening films that explore the "depth of the human soul" through the lens of:
* **Classical Literature:** Deep dives into the works of Mikhail Lermontov and Rasul Gamzatov.
* **Art & Philosophy:** Analyzing the visionary worlds of Mikhail Vrubel and the spiritual power of prayer.
* **Educational Reform:** Advocating for the status of the "Great Teacher" in the age of AI and global digital transformation.
