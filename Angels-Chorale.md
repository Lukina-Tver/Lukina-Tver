# The Angel's Chorale: A Reunion of Souls

> *"He remembered himself from the moment an angel carried his soul into this world of sorrow and tears."* — Nikas Safronov on M. Lermontov

---

## I. The Nature of Genius
Mikhail Lermontov was a unique visionary. Unlike many, he retained a spiritual memory of his soul's journey before birth. This "angelic memory" became the foundation of one of his most famous poems, **"The Angel"**.

### The Poem: "The Angel"
*Translated from Russian*

Across the midnight sky an angel flew,  
And a quiet song he sang as he withdrew;  
The moon, and the stars, and the clouds in a throng  
Hearkened unto that holy song.  

He carried a young soul in his embrace  
For the world of sorrow, the tears’ dwelling place;  
And the sound of his song in the soul so young  
Remained without words, but lived on and clung.

## II. A Living Connection: Tarkhany 200 Years Later
In a remarkable historical moment, the angelic soul of the poet reunited the **Lermontov** and **Arsenyev** lineages in Tarkhany. 

**Maria Korolyova**, a descendant of the Lermontov family, shares her thoughts:
> *"It is this feeling of unity—when you stand among your own... there is a connection between you spanning 200 or 300 years of genealogy."*
